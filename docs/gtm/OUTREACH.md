# Outreach Sequence — to the Activation Owner

Target: **VP Customer Success / Head of Onboarding / RevOps.** Lead with the **gap**,
never the features. Goal of every message: book a 25-minute conversation, not a
demo. Keep it short, specific, and curious.

> Rule: warm intros first (see `TARGET_ACCOUNTS.md`). Cold is the last resort.

## Touch 1 — Warm intro (best path)

**To the connector:**
> "Quick favor — are you still close with [Name] at [Company]? I'm researching how
> SaaS teams handle revenue that stalls between signing and activation (signed
> customers who never reach first value and skip the next invoice). Would love 25
> min to learn how [Company] handles it — not a pitch. Worth an intro?"

**Forwardable blurb:**
> "I'm looking into the gap between a signed contract and the second invoice — the
> accounts that stall in onboarding and quietly stop paying. Trying to learn how
> good teams catch and recover them. 25 min, purely to learn."

## Touch 1 — Cold email (if no warm path)

**Subject:** what happens between signed and activated at [Company]?

> Hi [First name],
>
> Quick research question, not a pitch: at [Company], when a customer signs but
> stalls in onboarding and never reaches first value — who owns that, and how do you
> know before they skip the next invoice?
>
> I'm studying how teams recover that "signed-but-not-activated" revenue and, more
> importantly, *prove* what was actually saved. If you've wrestled with it, I'd love
> 25 minutes to learn how you handle it.
>
> Worth a short call next week?
>
> [Name]

## Touch 2 — LinkedIn (2–3 days later)

> Hi [First name] — sent a note on email too. I'm researching the gap between signed
> and activated (the accounts that stall and skip the next invoice). Curious how
> [Company] catches them. Open to a 25-min learning chat?

## Touch 3 — Value nudge (4–5 days later)

> [First name] — last nudge. One thing I keep hearing: teams can *see* stalled
> accounts but can't prove how many they recovered *because of* an action vs would
> have come back anyway. If that's familiar, I can show you how to size the leak from
> a single CSV — no integration. Worth 25 minutes?

## Principles

- **Lead with the gap, end with a question.** No feature names, no "platform."
- **Ask to learn, not to sell** — the first call is discovery (`DISCOVERY_SCRIPT.md`).
- **One concrete hook:** "size the leak from one CSV, no integration."
- **Stop after 3 touches.** A non-reply is data; move to the next account.

## The handoff

Positive reply → book the call → run `DISCOVERY_SCRIPT.md`. If it lands, request the
CSV and run `ROI_CALCULATOR.md`; send `EXEC_ONE_PAGER.md` as the leave-behind.
