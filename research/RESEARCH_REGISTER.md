## Mission #001 — Global Capability Landscape (Revenue Recovery Lifecycle)

- **Mission ID:** #001 (incl. #001A adversarial evidence-completion layer)
- **Research question:** Does a product, platform, methodology, or combination already deliver the complete
  chain — Identify → Prioritize → Assign/Coordinate → Execute → Measure → Attribute → Prove Revenue Returned
  → Preserve Auditability?
- **Status:** Closed & **Frozen** — Canonical Baseline, Research Version 1.0 (supersede-only)
- **Freeze:** Frozen 2026-07-17 per Research Freeze Certificate. The canonical report is **read-only** and
  must never be silently rewritten. Changes require: (1) new first-party evidence, (2) a formal reopen under
  the documented Reopen Conditions, or (3) a superseding research version (v1.1+).
- **Canonical report:** `research/MISSION_001_GLOBAL_CAPABILITY_LANDSCAPE.md`
- **Major conclusions:**
  - C1 — No credible evidence of a generic, cross-domain software product supporting all eight stages
    independently.
  - C2 — Proof of returned revenue is primarily domain-specific or services-led (money-anchored via
    dispute/contingency; rigorous causal/holdout proof absent from recovery software).
  - C3 — A near-complete chain is assemblable from existing systems, but only with substantial integration,
    consulting, and manual attribution; the recovery-attribution-proof loop is native nowhere.
  - C4 (supporting) — Software "recovery/savings" numbers routinely blend Observed/Estimated/Forecast/Proven.
- **Confidence (conclusion-specific):** C1 High · C2 High · C3 Medium-High · C4 High.
- **Residual unknowns:** xfactrs beyond-detection (primary blocked, 403); Workday/Salesforce/Microsoft/
  SAP-Signavio internals; energy/utilities/marketplace/royalty verticals (survey-only); private/enterprise-
  internal systems; unannounced attribution/holdout/immutable-proof modules; proof internals of HighRadius/
  Waystar/Machinify; non-English/regional systems.
- **Reopen triggers:** a single generic cross-domain product with verified no-double-count attribution +
  holdout + immutable proof of collected returned revenue + auditor sign-off across ≥2 domains; primary
  (non-marketing) docs proving native holdout/tamper-evident recovery proof at a named vendor; a non-English
  or private-enterprise full-chain proof-loop; evidence buyers routinely operate a unified chain.
- **Next approved mission:** Mission #002 — **Pending definition and explicit approval.**
