## Pre-research position (bias firewall)
- **Expected answer:** dark mode helps mainly on OLED at high brightness; small at typical brightness;
  negligible on LCD → the universal "dark mode saves battery" is over-general.
- **What would change it:** a credible whole-device runtime measurement showing large savings at typical
  brightness, or material LCD savings.

## Purpose within Mission #005
This is a **data-readiness authoring pilot**, not a substantive research investment. The topic is small,
public, and low-stakes; its role is to prove native dual authoring + operational Stage A validation. Findings
are recorded honestly but are **not** an NH position on displays.
