## Findings (plain language — pilot record, not an NH position)
Organized four-day-week pilots exist and most participating firms continued (C1/C2); participants reported
large wellbeing gains (C4, self-reported). But the **causal** productivity claim is **Inconclusive** (C3):
signals are self-reported from self-selected firms, and rigorous objective-output evidence is limited (C6).
The universal "works everywhere" claim is only **Partially Supported** (C5, sector skew). An energy/carbon
benefit is **Unsupported** in the reviewed scope (C7).
